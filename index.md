I work in higher category theory and its applications to logic and computer
science. My interests include relative monads and pseudomonads, multicategories,
and the categorical semantics of type theory.

### Preprints and submitted work
{: .preprints}

- Idempotence for relative monads (with **[Nathanael Arkor](https://arkor.co/)**), 2025 — *submitted to Quaestiones Mathematicae* [ [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.17794) ]
- Bicategories of algebras for relative pseudomonads (with **[Nathanael Arkor](https://arkor.co/)** and **[Philip Saville](https://philipsaville.co.uk/)**), 2025 [ [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.12510) ]
